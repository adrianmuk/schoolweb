School Background

The school was started in 2006 with only one compus and that was mukono
at first the school had 40 staff members.
The founders of this school were;
MR Aloysious Musiime, MR Abitegeka Jackson, MR Mukisa Adrian, MR Kakooza Azizi,MS Dorothy Lunkuse

School Administrative Structure

Director

Mr. Kakooza Azizi
He holds a bachelors degree in law , PHD in arts with education
from makerere University.He sits on the schools board of governors he is responsible
for approval of policies made at school by the head teacher and his team. He
carries responsibility of ensuring students wel fare at the school.

Head teacher

Mr. Mukisa Adrian
He holds a bachelors degree in civil engneering , a PHD in science
with Education from makerere University and the University of Manchester in U.K
He is the head of the school, meaning that he undertakes the overall responsibility
in decision making and policy making at the school. he also takes part in the
approval of new teachers .

Deputy Head teacher

Mr. Jinno jackson Abitegeka.
He holds a masters degree in public Administration, PHD in science
with education. He is responsible for admitting new students to the school on
behalf of the head teacher.He is tasked to undertake collective responsibility at
school on behalf of the head teacher. The school bursar , the director of
studies report to him in case of any need to change the school pplicies and rules.
He also approves the teachers being employed at school.He is a cardinal
pillar in the policy making at the school.

Director of studies (D.O.S)

Mr. Aloysious Musiime
He holds a masters degree in Education and a bachelors degree in law from makerere
university. He is responsible for all academic issues at the school, he manages
all the school teaching staff plus the sports master. He is also responsible for designing class
time tables, cordinates the heads of departments and the class teachers
for the sake of excellence of students.

School bursar

Ms Dorothy Lunkuse.
She holds a masters degree in planning and management, and a PHD in business
administration from the miami University of Ohio in America. She is responsible for all finances
of a school, She handles the withdraw and deposit of school funds in banks.
