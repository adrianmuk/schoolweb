WELCOME TO SEETA HIGH SCHOOLS

Seeta High School opened its gates to the world in 2000. In its eighteen years of existence,
it has made its mark in the education history of the country as a Towering Academic Giant. It is located
in Mukono district. It is a mixed Boarding Secondary School with Ordinary and Advanced Levels
of education. It has three campuses; Main Campus in Seeta before Kigunga trading center, Mbalala campus on
Kampala – Jinja Highway (5 Km after Mukono town) and Green Campus 3kms along Kayunga – Bugerere road.
All our campuses have uniform policy and guidelines as we aim to pass out a uniform product. The school boasts
of a serene environment, state of the art facilities conducive for learning and is very accessible. We offer quality
education and talent development for all our learners.
